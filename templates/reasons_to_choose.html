Why Choose Browser-beijin?

An outline of the key differences

So many translation companies...

In a world full of translation companies, why choose Browser-beijin? In a nutshell, experience, focus, speed, and anonymity are the four strong points of Browser-beijin.

Provides translation done by a translator with a long work history in Japan (10 years in education / translation experience)

With other companies, you might not know who or where the translators are. Browser-beijin provides translation by a translator located in Japan, with years of business experience.

Gives translation done by one translator, working from a desktop PC

Other 'on demand' translation companies routinely route incoming translation to the smartphones of translators who are on-the-go, sometimes multiple translators at a time. Is your translator at home with the TV remote in one hand, translating your request on his smartphone with the other hand? With Browser-beijin, you can be sure translation is done by one focused professional working in a quiet office environment.

Offers on-demand (10-minute) translation with measurable, defined deadlines and an automatic-refund system

If you make a request between the hours of 9:30 and 17:45 Monday through Friday, if the translator is logged in, the answer will come back within 10 minutes. When you click the request button, a visible timer appears on your homescreen. If the answer doesn't come back in 10 minutes, you are automatically refunded the cost of translation, plus a bonus amount.

Is sensitive to the anonymity-needs of the Japanese market

Before beginning translation, other companies will ask for tons of information. Who wants to give out their company name, address, or mobile number? With Browser-beijin, all you must provide is a credit card number (not kept on file) and an email address if you'd like a receipt.
